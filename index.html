FAQ

What ares Lufis ?

Lufis Bath Party is a collection of 10,000 NFTs hand-drawn by Gal Shir. Lufis are randomly generated and constructed from over 200 different components. Each Lufi is unique and minted as an NFT on the Polygon blockchain. Lufis are dynamic NFTs and can change their traits over time by attending parties!

What the tech?

Lufis are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Polygon blockchain. After each party, the updated metadata and image will be uploaded to IPFS. The metadata includes all the historical versions of your Lufi, and all previous versions will be hosted on IPFS.
